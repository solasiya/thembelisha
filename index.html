Our Family’s Legacy

Thembelisha Day Care & Pre‑School is a family‑founded centre built on the love, vision, and dedication of my mother. We continue her dream by offering children a warm, home‑like environment filled with care, safety, and excellence. This is more than a school — it’s our family’s legacy of giving children a strong start in life.

Who We Are

Vision, Purpose & Mission

Vision

To raise children who feel deeply loved, supported, and empowered. We dream of a place where laughter fills the rooms, curiosity is encouraged, and every child’s uniqueness is celebrated.

Purpose

To give every child a strong, loving start in life. We aim to offer a nurturing space where children feel safe, respected, and encouraged to discover who they are through play and guided learning.

Mission

To raise confident, joyful, and capable young children through quality education rooted in love, safety, and respect. We strive to provide enriching learning experiences.
